Founded in 2017, Risolve was born from a shared love of printmaking, design and innovation.

Why Riso?

It's unique

There are multiple colors that are unique to the Riso process including fluorescent colors which are very difficult to create digitally and through other print methods.

It's environmentally friendly

The Risograph uses plant-based ink and the machines are incredibly energy efficient with a minimum amount of waste.

It's unique

Riso combines the best of the digital and traditional world: a hand-made look and feel with the speed, low-cost and efficiency of a modern printer.
